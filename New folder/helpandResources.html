1. Indoor Air Quality (IAQ)

HSE UK Standard
Why it Matters

Poor air quality in warehouses is often caused by forklift exhaust (carbon monoxide), dust from cardboard handling, and chemical vapors. Long-term exposure can lead to respiratory issues like asthma or chronic fatigue.

QuickShip Protocol: Ensure all loading dock doors remain open during heavy shipments and check CO monitors daily.

3. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

HSE Guidelines
The Last Line of Defense

PPE protects the user against health or safety risks at work. In QuickShip warehouses, the following are mandatory in designated zones:

  • High-Vis Vests: Required on all floor operations.
  • Steel-Toe Boots: Required for heavy lifting areas.
  • Safety Glasses: Required near packing machinery.

4. Record Keeping & Compliance

ISO 45001
Importance of Documentation

Accurate record keeping is vital for legal protection and identifying trends. We maintain records for:

  1. Accident logs (kept for minimum 3 years).
  2. Risk assessments.
  3. Equipment maintenance logs (forklifts/conveyors).
  4. Staff training certifications.

5. Workplace Violence Prevention

HSE Violence Info
Zero Tolerance Policy

Workplace violence includes physical assault, verbal abuse, harassment, or threats. Stress and high targets can sometimes lead to conflict. QuickShip maintains a strict Zero Tolerance Policy.

If you feel threatened or see harassment, report it to HR immediately via the anonymous channel.
