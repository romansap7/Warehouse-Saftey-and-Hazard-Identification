Emergency Planning Protocol
Critical Rule: Do not use elevators during a fire alarm. Use the marked stairs.
Action Checklist:
Know the Routes: Memorize the two nearest exits from your workstation.
Assembly Point A: Located at the North Parking Lot (Blue Sign).
Stop Work Immediately: When the alarm sounds, shut down machinery safely if time permits, then leave.

Materials Handling Guide
Manual Lifting Technique (The T.I.L.E. Principle):
  • Task: Does it involve twisting or stooping?
  • Individual: Is it too heavy for you? Ask for help.
  • Load: Is the load unstable or sharp?
  • Environment: Is the floor slippery?
Forklift Zone Rules:
Pedestrians must make eye contact with drivers before crossing aisles. Never assume they see you.

Automation & Robotics Safety

QuickShip uses AGVs (Automated Guided Vehicles) for pallet transport.

Safety with AGVs:
  1. Never stand in the "Yellow Tape" path of an AGV.
  2. Do not place obstacles (boxes/pallets) on the AGV track.
  3. If an AGV is stuck, do not push it. Call maintenance.
Sensor Blind Spots: Robots have sensors, but they cannot stop instantly. Maintain a 3-foot distance.

Hazardous Chemicals Protocol
Understanding SDS (Safety Data Sheets):

Every chemical in the warehouse has a corresponding SDS sheet located in the Red Binder at the Supervisor's desk. Read it BEFORE handling.

In Case of Spill:
1. Isolate: Cordon off the area immediately.
2. Protect: Put on chemical-resistant gloves and goggles.
3. Contain: Use the "Spill Kit" absorbent socks to stop spreading.

Electrical Hazards
Common Hazards to Report:
  • Overloaded power strips (Daisy chaining).
  • Frayed or damaged charging cables for scanners.
  • Water leaks near electrical panels.
Warning: Only certified electricians are allowed to open breaker panels. If you see an open panel, cordon it off and report it.

Lockout / Tagout (LOTO)
The 6 Steps of LOTO:
  1. Preparation: Notify affected employees.
  2. Shutdown: Turn off the machine normally.
  3. Isolation: Disconnect from energy source.
  4. Lockout/Tagout: Apply your personal lock and tag.
  5. Stored Energy Check: Release tension/pressure.
  6. Verification: Try to turn it on to ensure it's dead.

"One key, one lock, one person."
