Operational Guidelines

Core definitions for the QuickShip facility.

The Warehouse

A commercial building for storage of goods. In QuickShip, this is a controlled environment where efficiency meets strict inventory management protocols. Only authorized personnel are permitted on the floor.

Safety Protocols

A set of guidelines aimed at preventing injuries and incidents. This includes mandatory PPE usage, speed limits for forklifts, and ergonomic lifting techniques.

Key Rule: Stop work if conditions are unsafe.

Hazard Controls

The systematic process of identifying potential risks and applying controls.

  • Elimination: Remove the hazard.
  • Engineering: Isolate people from hazard.
  • Administrative: Change the way people work.

QuickShip Safety

QuickShip is a comprehensive warehouse safety management platform designed to protect workers, prevent incidents, and ensure compliance. Our mission is to create safer workplaces through technology.
