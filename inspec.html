360° View Training Guide

Welcome to the 360° Warehouse Inspection View! Here's how to navigate the environment:

Mouse Interaction
Click and drag your mouse anywhere in the 360° view to look around the warehouse. Move in any direction to explore.
Touch Controls (Mobile/Tablet)
On touch devices, simply swipe your finger across the screen to pan the camera and explore the environment.
Arrow Buttons
Use the left/right arrow buttons below the view to automatically pan the camera in that direction.
VR Mode
Click the VR button to enter virtual reality mode. Use a Google Cardboard or compatible VR headset for an immersive experience.
Fullscreen
Click the fullscreen button in the bottom-right corner of the 360° view to expand it for better visibility.
Tip: The 360° view uses A-Frame technology. For the best experience, ensure your browser supports WebGL and you have a stable internet connection.
